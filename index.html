We've got what you need!

Founded in Warsaw, Poland in January 1990, Couderq & Partners is an French-Polish corporate real estate consulting office that became one of the leading agencies on the Warsaw real estate market. It also became Ukrainian in 1996 with the development of a branch in Kiev where it is also recognized as one of the top service providers.

The company's team of experienced agents, architects and lawyers provide a broad range of cross-border real estate services while remaining fully dedicated to quality.

Our multicultural team, our strong research department and our careful approach to the different real estate sub-markets stand behind several success stories in Central Europe.

The care and attention given toward our clients have satisfied the requirements of over one half of the American and French Fortune 500 companies and over a third of the large German companies active in Poland and in the Ukraine.

At Your Service

Our work at C&P is based on a wide range of experience and an in-depth knowledge of the Polish and Ukrainian real estate markets. Over the past 18 years we have developed the knowledge and expertise needed to be able to fulfil the needs of the most demanding clients. With our individual approach where the Partners of the Firm are included in all levels of activity, with our multilingual team of brokers, lawyers, architects and researchers we are able to offer a wide range of corporate real estate consulting services. We strive to ensure maximum effectiveness in real estate consulting and negotiations, selling and leasing commercial property including retail and industrial space throughout the whole of Poland and Ukraine, architectural and legal advice as well as providing constantly updated market analysis and evaluations.
